// 01 — About

A brief
introduction

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence, where I research at the Robotics department. My work focuses on developing intelligent robotic systems in medical and underwater areas.

Previously, I was an ML Engineer at Fermilab and completed my PhD at Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, where my dissertation explored embedded real-time action recognition deep architectures for animal action recognition.

Beyond research, I am a big fun on adventures, reading, traveling and gym. I do not consider myself a nerd but...well...probably I am.
